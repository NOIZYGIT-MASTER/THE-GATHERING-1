All media enters one ocean: music, voice, film, scripts, lessons. The engine deconstructs each stream into tempo, emotion, structure, and semantics, then stores it as reusable intelligence for generation and teaching.

Video -> scene pacing and narrative patterns
Text -> semantic vectors and style signatures

Introduction

SHAPE THE HUMAN CHARACTER OF YOUR VOICE

  • BIOVOX: Modify physiological elements of the human vocal tract and manipulate vocal characteristics like nasality, formants, and vowel shapes for truly unique, human-sounding results.

NEW & IMPROVED EFFECTS

  • RING MOD: Add metallic texture and rhythmic movement to your vocal with the new hybrid Ring Mod/Tremolo effect.
  • CHORUS: Add lush layers to thicken and enhance your vocal.
  • SHRED: Slice and sequence your vocal with the completely revamped Shred effect.
  • EFFECTS CHAIN RE-ORDERING: Change the processing order in the effects chain quickly and easily by clicking and dragging any effect panel to a new position.

VISUALIZE PROCESSING & MANAGE YOUR VOCAL IN THE MIX

  • ANEMONE VISUALIZATION: A new and beautiful way to visualize and control VocalSynth’s effect on your vocal.
  • NEUTRON 2 VISUAL MIXER: Control volume, pan and width of any VocalSynth 2 instance in your session from the Neutron 2 Visual Mixer window. Learn more about the Neutron 2 Visual Mixer.
  • NEUTRON 2 MASKING METER: Select VocalSynth 2 instances in the Neutron 2 EQ Masking Meter to monitor and evaluate how your vocal is competing with other aspects of your mix. Learn more about the Masking Meter in Neutron 2.
  • TONAL BALANCE CONTROL: Select VocalSynth 2 instances in Tonal Balance Control to gain insight on how your vocal contributes to the overall tonal balance of your mix. Learn more about Neutron 2 Advanced & Tonal Balance Control.
  • INSIGHT 2: Select VocalSynth 2 instances as a source in the Insight 2 Spectrogram meter. Learn more about Insight 2.

DEEPER CONTROL & CUSTOMIZATION

  • MODULE ADVANCED VIEWS: VocalSynth 2 reveals a number of controls for sculpting and enhancing the sound of the different modules.
    • SYNTH CONTROLS: Customize the synthesizer engine controls in the Biovox, Vocoder, Compuvox, and Talkbox modules.
    • VOCODER BAND CONTROLS: Control the volume and pan of the Vocoder frequency bands.
    • MODULE MIX CONTROLS: Tailor the output of each module with the pan and filter controls in the Mix section of the module advanced views.

ENHANCED STEREO PROCESSING OPTIONS

  • GLOBAL PAN & WIDTH: Adjust the apparent stereo field and the stereo pan position of VocalSynth’s processed output.
  • MODULE PAN: Control the stereo pan position of each module’s output individually.

IMPROVEMENTS & OPTIMIZATIONS IN VOCALSYNTH 2

  • IMPROVED CPU & PERFORMANCE.
  • REFRESHED USER INTERFACE: Check out the Interface Guide for an overview of the VocalSynth 2 interface.
  • SOUND QUALITY ENHANCEMENTS:
    • Completely updated Vocoder modes for smoother, warmer sounding processing.
    • Internal signal flow updates to optimize the output of each module.

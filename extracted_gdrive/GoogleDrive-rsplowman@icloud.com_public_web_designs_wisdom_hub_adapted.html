Why Both?
What Each Does The Other Cannot

Claude Max 20x and Cohere Enterprise are not redundant. They are architecturally complementary — each covering the territory the other cannot.

🧠
Reasoning vs. Retrieval
Claude Max 20x reasons — it generates, synthesises, writes, and decides. Cohere Enterprise retrieves — it finds the right knowledge from a massive corpus with precision. Together: generate with context.
🔒
Cloud vs. Sovereign
Claude Max 20x runs on Anthropic's infrastructure. Cohere Enterprise runs on yours — VPC, on-prem, or air-gapped. Dr. Benoit's knowledge never leaves your controlled environment. Minister and SSHRC require this.
🌍
English vs. 70+ Languages
Claude handles English at the highest level. Cohere's Tiny Aya handles 70+ languages offline — Cree, Ojibwe, Japanese, Swahili, French — without internet. The international expansion requires both.
⚡
Creativity vs. Scale
Claude Max 20x writes a grant proposal. Cohere Compass instantly finds the 12 most relevant Dr. Benoit insights to support it — from 24 sessions of tagged knowledge — in seconds. Creativity + precision.
◈
Thinking vs. Memory
Claude Max 20x has a 1M token context window — extraordinary, but session-limited. Cohere's knowledge graph is permanent, growing, and searchable across all 24 sessions simultaneously. Thinking + persistent memory.
🤝
Two Partner Stories

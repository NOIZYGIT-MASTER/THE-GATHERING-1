## What NOIZYKIDZ Is

NOIZYKIDZ is haptic music education for children who are typically excluded from music: deaf kids, kids on the autism spectrum, kids with sensory differences. Its design standards — deaf-first and autism-calm — are inherited by every other brand in the MC96 ecosystem.

If the rest of the empire forgets who it's for, NOIZYKIDZ is what reminds it.

---

## Design Principles

| Principle | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **Deaf-first** | Every audio experience has a haptic equivalent. Sound is optional, never required. |
| **Autism-calm** | No flashing, no sudden loud, no forced social — calibrated sensory envelopes. |
| **Child-consent** | Guardian-mediated consent flows. Never Clauses apply doubly to minors. |
| **Joy-driven** | Learning through play, not drill. Frequency as a friend, not a test. |

Tips & Examples

Languages:

As part of our look into Neutron’s General Functions, here are a few humble words of advice to consider as you mix:

  • To avoid over-processing, use the module Mix sliders in the module chain to blend in some dry, unprocessed signal.
  • Remember that Track Assistant results are not final settings, treat them as a starting point for your mix.
  • When using the Compressor modules, try starting with one band and add more bands as needed.
  • Use two EQ nodes in Dynamic Mode to control the sibilant areas of your track.
  • Analysis can be great — but it has to sound good too, so don’t forget to A/B.
  • Try blending the Exciter modes, such as: Tube/Tape on vocals or Tape/Retro on electric guitars.
  • Unmask a mix by comparing all other tracks against the lead vocal.
  • Compression Ratios are important. High ratios may suck the life out of a mix.
  • Well defined kick drum and bass relationships are the bedrock of any groove.
  • Use the EQ’s Learn function to help identify problem areas in your track.

The Stack 
LAYER 1 — THE EAR (Librosa)
  ├── Onset Detection         → Knows exactly when beats hit. Generated audio locks to the grid.
  ├── BPM / Tempo Tracking    → Live tempo map. Sync generated audio to any live track.
  ├── Chroma / Key / Mode     → D minor = dread. G major = triumph. Voice shifts register to match.
  ├── Spectral Centroid       → Detects brightness and energy. Knows when music is building vs releasing.
  └── MFCC Fingerprinting     → Timbre signature. Voice that sounds like it belongs in this world.

LAYER 2 — THE BRAIN (Claude API)
  ├── Receives signal analysis from Layer 1
  ├── Translates acoustic data → semantic meaning
  └── "BPM 140, minor key, rising centroid = high tension, approaching climax, dread register"

Using RX as an audio editor with Adobe Premiere Pro CC

  1. Inside of Premiere, right-click on an audio clip in your timeline and select "Reveal in Finder" (OS X) or "Reveal in Explorer" (Windows).
  2. Open the resulting file in RX 5 Audio Editor.
  3. Perform the required audio edit, and then click File>Overwrite Original File

Note: If you have Adobe Audition installed as well, you can right-click on an audio clip in your timeline, and select "Edit Clip In Adobe Audition." Then follow the steps listed above for using RX Connect with Adobe Audition CC.

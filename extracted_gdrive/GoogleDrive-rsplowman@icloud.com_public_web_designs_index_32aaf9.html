What’s new in Neutron 2?

CONTROL AND VISUALIZE THE TONAL BALANCE OF YOUR MUSIC

  • Tonal Balance Control: Access all the controls from Ozone and Neutron’s Equalizers remotely. View the dynamic range of your low-end. Import custom tracks to create your own targets.

PLACE ELEMENTS OF YOUR MIX IN A VIRTUAL SPACE

  • Visual Mixer: Easily view the sonic activity of every track in your mix. Control volume, pan, and stereo width of any Neutron 2 module or plug-in.

DIAL IN THE PERFECT SETTING FOR REMOVING BLEED AND NOISE FROM YOUR MUSIC

  • Gate: Three bands of iZotope’s Gating DSP, complete with advanced side-chain capabilities, crossover learn, and the brand new Hysteresis and Hold DSP.

CONNECT YOUR TRACKS TO VISUAL MIXER, TONAL BALANCE CONTROL, AND MASKING METER

  • Mix Tap: A lightweight, purpose built utility plug-in with inter-plugin communication technology, channel ops, delay offset, HPF, volume, pan and stereo imaging.

IMPROVEMENTS & OPTIMIZATIONS IN NEUTRON 2

  • Track Assistant: A starting point for your mix with improved intelligence, more creative control, and a newly refined user experience.
  • Soft Saturation: Inspired by a British-style inductor EQ and designed to introduce non-linear harmonic saturation at the input of the Neutron Equalizer.
  • I/O Section Improvements: Stereo Width, Panning, Sum to Mono, Swap Channels, Delay Offset and Polarity Invert.

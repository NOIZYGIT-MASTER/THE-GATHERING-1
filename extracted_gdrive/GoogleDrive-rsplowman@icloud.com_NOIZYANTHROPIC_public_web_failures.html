How We Handle Failures

Our Philosophy

Every system fails eventually. What matters is how you respond. At NOIZY, we believe failures are opportunities to demonstrate competence, transparency, and care for the people who trust us.

"We don't hide from failures. We mechanically prevent the worst ones, and publicly account for the rest."

When Things Go Wrong

When our automated systems detect an anomaly, here's what happens:

🔍

1. Automatic Detection

Our systems detect anomalies within seconds through real-time monitoring and error budgets.

🛑

2. Automatic Halt

Traffic to the affected version is stopped immediately. No human decision required.

🔬

3. Root Cause Analysis

We investigate what happened, without blame. A postmortem draft is auto-generated.

✅

4. Fix & Verify

We fix the issue and verify through the same gradual deployment process.

📝

5. Public Postmortem

We publish what happened, what we learned, and what we changed. No hiding.

Our Principles

PRINCIPLE 01
Blameless Culture
We focus on systems, not individuals. Every postmortem asks "what allowed this to happen?" not "who caused this?"
PRINCIPLE 02
Public Accountability
Significant incidents get public postmortems. We believe transparency builds more trust than silence ever could.
PRINCIPLE 03
Prevention Over Detection
The best incident is the one that never reaches you. We invest heavily in automated safeguards that catch issues before deployment.
PRINCIPLE 04
Never Compromise Trust
Your voice, your consent, your data — these are never at risk. Our systems are designed to fail closed, protecting you even when things go wrong.

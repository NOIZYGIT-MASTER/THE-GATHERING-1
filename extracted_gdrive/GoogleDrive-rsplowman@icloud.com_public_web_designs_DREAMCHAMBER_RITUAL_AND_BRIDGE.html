Teams Bridge — Real-World Checklist
  • A
    Join the same meeting from both devices.
    Create a meeting link in Teams. Open it on iPad and iPhone. The link opens in the Teams app if installed. Join from Calendar or paste the link directly.
  • B
    Grant camera/mic permissions on iPad.
    First time joining, Teams will prompt for mic and camera access. Grant once — it remembers. Settings → Privacy → Microphone/Camera → Teams if needed.
  • C
    Screen share from iPad (optional cockpit view).
    Enable Screen Recording in Control Center first (Settings → Control Center → add Screen Recording). In the Teams meeting, tap Share → Share Screen.
  • D
    Bridge is live.
    iPhone captures voice. iPad shows the room. GOD runs the engine. Teams is the synapse. The nervous system is wired.

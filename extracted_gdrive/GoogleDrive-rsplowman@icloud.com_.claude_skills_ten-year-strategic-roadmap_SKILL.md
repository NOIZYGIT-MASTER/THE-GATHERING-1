### 2. Cloudflare Platform Risk
**Risk**: Cloudflare changes terms, becomes unreliable, or is acquired.
**Impact**: Complete infrastructure collapse (Heaven down).
**Mitigation**:
- Multi-cloud strategy by Year 3 (AWS, Google Cloud redundancy)
- Data portability: all D1 data exportable within 24 hours
- Fallback: on-premise Heaven on GOD.local always operational

### 3. Hostile Acquisition Attempt
**Risk**: Major tech company (Google, Meta, Apple) tries to buy NOIZY.
**Impact**: Loss of independence, creator trust destroyed.
**Mitigation**:
- Guild Assembly given veto power (50%+1 vote kills any sale)
- Poison pill: creator data becomes public domain if acquired
- Capitalization: keep majority ownership with RSP_001 + employee trust

### 4. Regulatory Prohibition of AI Voice Synthesis
**Risk**: Government bans voice synthesis entirely (extreme scenario).
**Impact**: Market collapses, NOIZY's core business disappears.
**Mitigation**:
- Pivot to consent-as-service (selling protection framework to other industries)
- Legal challenge strategy: pre-positioned with constitutional law teams
- Precedent: free speech protection for artist identity

### 5. Technology Renders Watermarking Obsolete
**Risk**: New synthesis method defeats C2PA, watermarking, all detection.
**Impact**: Consent enforcement impossible, system becomes symbolic.
**Mitigation**:
- Blockchain anchoring (immutable record of consent moment)
- Legal frameworks (contract law, not tech alone)
- Guild power: collective action, public pressure, cease-and-desist swarms
- Shift from detection to enforcement (legal consequences, not tech)

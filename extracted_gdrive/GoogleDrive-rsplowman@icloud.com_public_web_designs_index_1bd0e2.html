Introduction

Languages

Welcome to Nectar Elements, the latest addition to iZotope’s family of intelligent processing tools. Focused on overcoming the sometimes daunting and time-consuming task of achieving professional-sounding vocals, Nectar Elements aims to simplify your starting point with the introduction of Vocal Assistant. Vocal Assistant streamlines the process of getting a vocal to sit in your mix. It analyzes your vocal and provides customized, intelligent settings with scale controls in an easy-to-use interface.

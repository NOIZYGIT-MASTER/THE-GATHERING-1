## WHAT NOIZYKIDZ IS

A free, open-access music education program built so every child can participate — regardless of hearing, regardless of neurotype, regardless of budget. Haptic-first because music is vibration before it's sound.

## THE PROMISES

1. **Deaf-first design.** If a lesson doesn't work without audio, it doesn't ship. Sound is the bonus layer, not the primary layer.
2. **Autism-calm environments.** Soft-transition visuals, no surprise sounds, predictable structure. Sensory load is dialed down by default.
3. **Every kid plays.** No fee. No gatekeeping. No required hardware beyond what the program provides.
4. **Universal funding.** 1% of all NOIZY royalties, across every brand, every transaction, every territory — routed to NOIZYKIDZ trust. Irremovable. Forever.

## THE CURRICULUM

Built in partnership with DREAM (the creative partner agent). Uses:

- **HapticComposer** (DreamChamber module) — translates audio intent into haptic patterns.
- **Simple visual notation** — color + shape + vibration, not standard music notation.
- **Voice + gesture capture** — kids create, not just consume.
- **No grades, no tests, no comparison** — creative state over measurement.
- **Sessions are 20 minutes max** — attention, not endurance.

The 1% trust exists because:

- Rob grew up around kids who wanted to make music but couldn't afford lessons.
- Rob learned — late — how deaf children are often placed in music programs designed by hearing adults for hearing kids, where they're told they can participate "as an exception."

# NOIZYVOX Product Doctrine

## Summary

This document defines how NOIZYVOX should serve artists better than generic platform-renter voice systems.

It converts creator-first philosophy into product rules.

## Core Thesis

NOIZYVOX is not a voice vending machine.

It is a creator-governed identity system where voice, consent, direction, licensing, and recurring value remain linked to the human source.

## Product Rules

### 1. Approve Humans, Not Anonymous Uploads

The primary object is the human creator, not a detached model file.

Every voice twin must remain linked to a verified source.

### 2. Capture Essence, Not Generic Corpora

Intake should not feel like a sterile sentence-reading exam.

Capture should happen through modes and lived expression:

- calm
- story
- focus
- sleep
- command
- intimacy

When appropriate, DreamChamber capture can use guided prompts, sensory anchors, and emotional framing rather than random text.

### 3. Consent Must Be Enforced At Generation Time

Allowed and disallowed contexts are not legal footnotes.

They are generation constraints.

Examples:

- education allowed
- political use blocked
- adult content blocked
- implied endorsement blocked

### 4. First-Take Integrity Matters

NOIZYVOX should preserve the first read as a protected performance artifact.

Direction exists to create resonance, not to over-correct instinct out of the take.

### 5. Local-First Direction Boundaries Matter

Sensitive session capture should default to a local performance boundary.

The M2 Ultra hub model remains valid:

- actor-to-director by default
- optional silent client monitor
- local archive before cloud sync

### 6. Artists Participate In Ongoing Value

The economic model must remain usage-based and recurring.

One-time buyout logic is not the default doctrine.

### 7. Policy, Not Popularity, Governs Use

Every voice must support:

- pause controls
- revocation by context
- revocation by buyer where supported
- successor or retirement rules

### 8. The Product Must Offer Workflow, Not Just Output

To remain defensible, NOIZYVOX must solve a full workflow:

- capture
- direction
- licensing
- generation
- receipts
- audit
- enforcement

### 9. The Public Surface Must Match The Product

The public web layer must tell the truth about the system.

Marketing, docs, pricing, legal, and trust pages should expose:

- what the product is for
- what it is not for
- what safety constraints exist
- what consent limits apply
- what governance and review structures exist

## System Layers

### Intake Layer

- creator identity verification
- mode-based capture
- artist confirmation that the twin feels like them

### Governance Layer

- allowed contexts
- restricted contexts
- pricing tiers
- revocation controls
- successor settings

### Transmission Layer

- AVA direction packets
- actor-facing signal cards
- first-take protection
- NOIZYFISH coherence after capture

### Marketplace Layer

- curated casting instead of generic search
- voice cards that explain fit, limits, and modes
- usage receipts instead of black-box platform accounting

### Public Surface Layer

- sitecopy that explains product branches clearly
- legal and privacy language that matches real behavior
- trust and security language grounded in actual controls
- community and evidence pages that avoid overclaiming

### Enforcement Layer

- consent checks before generation
- audit logs
- evidence-pack support
- future detection and takedown routing

## Non-Negotiables

- creators are sources, not disposable suppliers
- consent is architectural
- recurring value must remain visible
- revocation must be real
- product language must avoid fake ownership claims over third-party infrastructure

## Success Condition

NOIZYVOX is working when the artist feels more legible, more protected, and more economically alive after entering the system, not less.

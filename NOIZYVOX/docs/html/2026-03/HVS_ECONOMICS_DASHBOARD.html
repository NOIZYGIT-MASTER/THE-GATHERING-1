Kill Switch Economics
When an artist revokes consent, the $1,000 revocation fee is charged to the LICENSEE — not the artist. The artist pays $0 to say no. Ever. 50% of the revocation fee covers NOIZY.AI's decommissioning costs. 50% goes to the union fund. The cost of saying no falls on the entity that profited from the Descendant.

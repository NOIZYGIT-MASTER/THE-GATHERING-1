Master Blueprint
Guiding Principles
🛡️
Principle I
Consent & Sovereignty
Communities hold ultimate authority over their knowledge. Nothing is digitized, shared, or monetized without informed, ongoing consent.
📚
Principle II
Full Context Preservation
Cultural knowledge is preserved with its full ceremonial, ecological, and linguistic context — not extracted or decontextualized.
🌱
Principle III
Ethical Amplification
Amplification only happens when communities choose it. Revenue, credit, and attribution always flow back to the knowledge source.
🤝
Principle IV
Cultural Respect
Engagement is approached with cultural humility. Sacred knowledge that communities wish to keep private remains private, always.

Google Workspace (Professional Email)

RECOMMENDED
Current setup: Cloudflare Email Routing forwards incoming mail to iCloud. This means you can RECEIVE email at rob@noizylab.ca, but you CANNOT SEND email as rob@noizylab.ca. Google Workspace solves this and gives you Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Meet, and more under your own domain.

Recommended Setup

Sign up at workspace.google.com
Business Starter ($7/user/month) is sufficient for most needs. Start with noizylab.ca as the primary domain. You get 30GB storage, custom email, and all Google apps.
Verify domain ownership
Add a TXT record to noizylab.ca DNS on Cloudflare. Google will provide the exact record. Verification is usually instant since your DNS is on Cloudflare.
Update MX records on Cloudflare
Replace Cloudflare Email Routing MX records with Google's single MX record:

MX smtp.google.com (priority 1)

This will disable Cloudflare Email Routing for that domain. That's expected — Google Workspace handles everything now.
Set up SPF, DKIM, and DMARC
SPF: v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all
DKIM: Generate in Google Admin Console > Apps > Gmail > Authenticate email
DMARC: v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; rua=mailto:rob@noizylab.ca
Add alias domains (FREE)
Add fishmusicinc.com, noizy.ai, noizyvox.com as alias domains. This lets you send/receive as rob@fishmusicinc.com etc. at no extra cost. Up to 20 alias domains included.
Create email aliases
Set up group aliases: help@, support@, repairs@, info@ — these are free and don't count as users. They forward to your main inbox.
Important: Kill the M365 tenant on GoDaddy Account #4 (fishmusicinc.com) BEFORE setting up Google Workspace to avoid MX record conflicts.

Transfer Steps (per domain)

Unlock the domain at GoDaddy
GoDaddy > My Products > Domain > Domain Settings > Domain lock: OFF
Get the authorization/EPP code
GoDaddy > Domain Settings > Transfer > Get authorization code. Save this code — you'll need it for Cloudflare.
Disable WHOIS privacy (temporarily)
Some transfers require WHOIS privacy to be off. You can re-enable it after transfer. Cloudflare includes free WHOIS privacy.
Initiate transfer at Cloudflare
Cloudflare Dashboard > Domain Registration > Transfer > Enter domain and auth code. Pay the transfer fee (usually just the renewal cost at Cloudflare's wholesale price).
Confirm transfer email
GoDaddy will send a confirmation email. Approve ASAP to speed up the transfer. If you don't act, transfer completes automatically after 5 days.
Verify DNS after transfer
Once transferred, set up MX records, SPF, DKIM, and DMARC for the new domain on Cloudflare.

## Core Philosophy

### Guiding Principles
1. **For All Humanity** - Research benefits should be accessible
2. **Do No Harm** - Safety first in all applications
3. **Evidence-Based** - Ground work in research
4. **Respect Diversity** - Individual differences matter
5. **Open Knowledge** - Share findings for collective benefit

### Research Ethics
- Informed consent for all studies
- Vulnerable population protections
- No pseudoscience claims
- Transparent methodology
- Accessible results

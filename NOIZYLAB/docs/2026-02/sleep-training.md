# SLEEP-TRAIN: Sleep Calming Techniques & Mind Training

**Research Code:** SLEEP-TRAIN
**Status:** Active Research
**Parent:** NOIZY.AI Research Division

---

## Mission

Research and develop auditory techniques to train the mind to understand and achieve states of calm, particularly for improved sleep quality and sleep onset.

---

## Core Research Areas

### 1. Brainwave States & Sleep
- Delta waves (0.5-4 Hz) - Deep sleep
- Theta waves (4-8 Hz) - Light sleep, meditation
- Alpha waves (8-12 Hz) - Relaxation, pre-sleep
- Transition patterns from waking to sleep

### 2. Audio-Induced Calm Training
- Progressive relaxation soundscapes
- Guided audio meditation
- Body scan audio techniques
- Breathing pattern entrainment

### 3. Sleep Onset Enhancement
- Pre-sleep audio routines
- Circadian rhythm audio cues
- Blue hour soundscapes
- Consistent bedtime audio signals

### 4. Mind Training for Calm
- Associative learning (sound = calm)
- Conditioned relaxation responses
- Habitual calm state access
- Stress response interruption

---

## Sleep Architecture Understanding

| Stage | Brainwave | Description | Audio Approach |
|-------|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| Awake | Beta | Alert, active | Transition down |
| Relaxed | Alpha | Calm, eyes closed | Pink noise, nature |
| Light Sleep | Theta | Drowsy, drifting | Binaural theta |
| Deep Sleep | Delta | Restorative | Very low frequency |
| REM | Mixed | Dreaming | Maintain, don't disrupt |

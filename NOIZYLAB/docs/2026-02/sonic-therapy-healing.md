- Schumann resonance (7.83Hz) applications

### 2. Tonal Therapy
- Pure tone applications
- Harmonic relationships
- Interval therapy
- Resonance patterns

### 3. Sound Healing Modalities
- Binaural beats
- Isochronic tones
- Monaural beats
- ASMR therapeutic applications

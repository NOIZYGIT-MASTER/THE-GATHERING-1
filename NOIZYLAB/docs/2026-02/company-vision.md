## The Grand Vision

### NOIZY.AI as a Destination

NOIZY.AI will be a **physical destination** - a Music & Film Museum and Creative School where:

- **Students** come to learn music, film, and creative technology
- **The creatively curious** discover inspiration and knowledge
- **All music & film knowledge** lives in one place
- **The Composers Vault** preserves R.S. Plowman's complete musical legacy
- **Research** advances auditory science for humanity's benefit

### The Mothership Concept

NOIZY.AI serves as the **mothership** - the central hub connecting:
- Creative education (School)
- Cultural preservation (Museum)
- Artist archives (The Composers Vault)
- Scientific research (AUDIO-HUMAN program)
- Technology innovation (AI Lab)
- Revenue operations (NOIZYLAB)
- Creative production (NOIZYFISH)
- Rights management (FISH MUSIC INC)

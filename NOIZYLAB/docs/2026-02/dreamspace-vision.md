## What is a Dreamspace?

A Dreamspace is more than a museum, more than a school, more than an archive. It's a **liminal creative zone** where:

- **Past meets future** — Understanding history unlocks tomorrow
- **Learning becomes creating** — Knowledge transforms into action
- **Sound meets vision** — Audio and video technology merge
- **Individual meets collective** — Personal creation joins larger legacy

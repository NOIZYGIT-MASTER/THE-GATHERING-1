## 1. NOIZYVOX — The Creator Data Union

**Mission:** Give creators collective control over their voice, identity, and economic destiny.

**What it is:** A union — not a platform — where creators register their voice sovereignty, grant scoped consent, earn 75% of revenue, and revoke permissions at any time.

**Target audience:** Musicians, voice actors, podcasters, audiobook narrators, any creator whose voice has economic value in the AI age.

**Core capabilities:**
- Voice Estate registration (acoustic fingerprint, governance rules, heirs)
- NCP consent management (grant, modify, revoke — machine-enforced)
- Real-time royalty dashboard (75/25 split, automated payouts)
- One-click revocation (1-hour enforcement SLA)
- Data portability (export everything, zero lock-in)
- Guild membership (collective defense, democratic governance)

**Technical stack:**
- Consent Gateway Worker (10-check decision matrix)
- D1 database (10 tables — consent, provenance, royalties, audit)
- Cloudflare KV (caching, rate limiting)
- R2 (Voice DNA storage — pending enablement)

**Revenue model:** 25% platform share on every usage event, plus premium membership tiers for advanced features.

**HVS Roadmap alignment:** Phases 1-3 (Identity → Consent → Economic layer)

## 2. NOIZYLAB — The Repair Layer

**Mission:** Fix what the extraction economy broke. Restore provenance, attribution, and compensation to existing AI-generated content.

**What it is:** A forensic and remediation service that identifies AI-generated voice content, traces its origins, and establishes proper consent and compensation chains.

**Target audience:** Record labels, streaming platforms, content distributors, legal teams investigating voice IP violations.

**Core capabilities:**
- Deepfake detection (spectral analysis, acoustic fingerprint matching)
- Provenance reconstruction (tracing unauthorized voice use back to source)
- Consent gap identification (finding voice use without NCP records)
- Remediation pathways (establishing retroactive consent or issuing takedowns)
- Expert witness documentation (forensic reports for legal proceedings)

**Technical stack:**
- Librosa + custom spectral analysis
- Voice DNA vault (R2 storage + acoustic fingerprint database)
- C2PA manifest verification
- GABRIEL agent dispatch for automated scanning

**Revenue model:** Per-investigation fees, platform licensing for bulk scanning, retainer agreements with labels/distributors.

**Strategic value:** NOIZYLAB creates demand for NOIZYVOX. Every investigation that reveals unauthorized voice use is a case study for why creators need consent infrastructure.

## 3. NOIZYKIDZ — Haptic Learning Through Sound

**Mission:** Teach children to create and appreciate music through tactile, sensory-rich experiences that respect consent from day one.

**What it is:** A haptic music education platform where kids learn by feeling sound — vibrations, textures, spatial audio — with built-in consent awareness as a foundational concept.

**Target audience:** Children 4-12, parents, educators, music teachers.

**Core capabilities:**
- Haptic feedback integration (vibration patterns mapped to musical elements)
- Sensory-first learning (feel rhythm before reading notation)
- Creator attribution from lesson one (every sample used is attributed)
- "My Voice" module (children record and own their voice identity from the start)
- Age-appropriate consent education ("This is YOUR voice. No one uses it without asking you.")

**Design philosophy:**
- Consent is taught as a fundamental concept, not an afterthought
- Every sound used in education carries provenance metadata
- Children learn that creative identity has value and deserves protection
- Play is the pedagogy — no screens-first, haptics-first

**Technical stack:**
- Web Audio API + haptic device integration
- Simplified NCP (kid-friendly consent interface)
- Parent/guardian consent proxy (adults authorize on behalf of minors)
- Content library (all licensed, all attributed, all consented)

**Revenue model:** Subscription (family/school plans), hardware partnerships (haptic devices), curriculum licensing to schools.

**Strategic value:** Generational play. Children who grow up understanding voice sovereignty become adults who demand it. NOIZYKIDZ is the 20-year pipeline.

## 4. LIFELUV — The Personal Mirror

**Mission:** Preserve the voices and stories of people you love — with their explicit consent — as inheritable digital assets.

**What it is:** A personal voice preservation platform where families capture loved ones' voices, stories, and acoustic identities with full consent, governance, and estate planning.

**Target audience:** Families, caregivers, estate planners, anyone who wants to preserve a loved one's voice with dignity and legal protection.

**Core capabilities:**
- Guided voice capture sessions (structured interviews, storytelling prompts)
- Voice Estate creation for non-musicians (every person has voice sovereignty)
- Estate governance (heirs, delegates, activation conditions)
- "Memory Capsule" — curated voice recordings with provenance and consent chain
- Grief-tech ethics (explicit boundaries on post-mortem voice use)

**Consent model:**
- The person whose voice is captured must provide consent (NCP)
- If the person is deceased, consent must have been established while alive
- No posthumous voice synthesis without explicit pre-mortem authorization
- Estate governance rules determine post-mortem permissions

**Technical stack:**
- Voice capture app (mobile-first)
- Voice Estate v1.0 schema (delegates, heirs, governance_json)
- R2 storage (encrypted voice samples)
- NCP consent records (scoped to family/personal use)

**Revenue model:** Premium storage tiers, estate planning integration, partnership with funeral/memorial services.

**Strategic value:** LIFELUV extends voice sovereignty beyond creators to every human being. It normalizes the concept that your voice is your property, forever.

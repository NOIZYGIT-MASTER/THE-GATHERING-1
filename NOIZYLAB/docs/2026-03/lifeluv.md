# LIFELUV

## Summary

LIFELUV is the long-term support framework that turns protected human creativity into durable, auditable, recurring value.

## Working Definition

LIFELUV means love translated into livelihood without surrendering ownership of the creative source.

## Core Functions

- Protect the creator
- Route valid revenue events
- Preserve attribution and consent
- Keep creative assets portable and inheritable

## Revenue Surfaces

- Works and releases
- Training licenses
- Inference revenue
- Mentor models
- Curation and taste channels
- Artifact editions
- Estate and beneficiary routing

## Constraints

- Payout logic must follow rights, not replace them.
- Biometric and identity data require explicit governance.
- Platform exports need to align with disclosure regimes.

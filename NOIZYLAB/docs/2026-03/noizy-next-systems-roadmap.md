# NOIZY Next Systems Roadmap

## Summary

This is the priority order for the next major NOIZY systems.

The rule is simple:

do not build six futures at once.
Build the dependency chain.

## Recommendation

Tackle the **DreamChamber VR/AR Prototype** first.

Reason:

- the global sonic map and timeline already exist as canon
- the school already has a curriculum and experiential model
- the prototype is the first place where world biome logic, spatial audio, ritual pacing, adaptive creation, and AI collaboration can be proven together
- the Flow-State Engine depends on a real interaction environment
- the Community Platform, Legacy Vault, and full School platform all need a working world to orbit around

## Priority Order

### 1. DreamChamber VR/AR Prototype

Build first.

Target:

- one playable spatial environment
- one geography-first path
- one feeling-first path
- one guided Listening Chamber mode

Core modules:

- biome browser
- spatial scene composer
- session state router
- first-take/session capture

Definition of done:

- a user can enter a sound biome
- move through space and alter sound intentionally
- switch between at least three state routes
- save one coherent session artifact

### 2. Global Sonic Map And Timeline

Already underway in canon.

Next step is not more prose.
Next step is machine-readable structure.

Target:

- biome matrix v0.1
- state-route schema
- lineage note schema

Why second:

- it feeds the prototype directly
- it becomes curriculum data
- it structures future vault and community systems

### 3. Flow-State Engine And Adaptive AI Composer

Build after the prototype has a real interaction loop.

Target:

- motion, breath, or gesture-driven state modulation
- adaptive musical response
- sober-first state design

Why third:

- sensor and state logic should be tuned inside a real environment, not in abstraction

### 4. Global Music School Platform

The curriculum exists.
The platform can follow the prototype.

Target:

- course pathways
- DreamChamber-linked lessons
- school rituals
- student progression tracking

Why fourth:

- the school becomes much stronger once students can enter a real world, not just read about one

### 5. Noizy Empire Community Platform

Build once there is a world, curriculum, and session artifacts worth sharing.

Target:

- circles
- mentorship
- live co-creation rooms
- archive-linked collaboration

Why fifth:

- communities form around meaningful activity, not around empty platform surfaces

### 6. Human Sonic Legacy Vault

Build after the system is generating enough valuable, lineage-aware artifacts to preserve.

Target:

- release archive
- lineage records
- performance preservation
- creator continuity and legacy modes

Why sixth:

- the vault should preserve living outputs from a proven system, not speculative categories alone

## Immediate Build Scope

The first prototype should be small and defensible:

- `Mode 1`: Listening Chamber
- `Mode 2`: World Biome Entry
- `Mode 3`: Spatial Composition Sketch

Use these input layers:

- Quest hand tracking
- head movement
- controller fallback
- optional local Nerve-to-Note hooks later

Use these output goals:

- state-aware audio routing
- biome transitions
- session capture
- exportable artifact for archive or teaching use

## Non-Negotiables

- sober-first prototype
- lineage-aware naming
- no fake clinical claims
- local-first handling for sensitive input
- human co-creation remains central

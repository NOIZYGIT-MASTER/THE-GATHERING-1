# NOIZY Empire Systems Blueprint

## Summary

This is the actionable master blueprint document for the NOIZY Empire ecosystem.

Use it as the top systems handoff for Claude, collaborators, or future product work.

It answers:

1. what the full ecosystem is
2. which modules already exist
3. how they depend on each other
4. what to build next

This is not the philosophy document.
That role belongs to [noizy-empire-master-blueprint.md](./noizy-empire-master-blueprint.md).

This is the execution-layer map.

For the shortest handoff version, use [noizy-empire-claude-build-brief.md](./noizy-empire-claude-build-brief.md).

## North Star

NOIZY exists to restore joy, love, passion, lineage, and human creative sovereignty through:

- sound
- ritual
- immersive environments
- adaptive instruments
- participatory education
- creator protection
- community
- long-term continuity

The system should feel like:

- a music school
- a temple of attention
- a world-scale instrument
- a culture-preserving archive
- a creator economy with dignity

## System Stack

The full ecosystem depends on eight connected layers:

1. Knowledge Engine
2. Global Sonic Map And Timeline
3. DreamChamber XR Runtime
4. Flow-State Engine And Adaptive Composer
5. Music School And Curriculum Layer
6. Community Platform
7. Human Sonic Legacy Vault
8. Rights, Provenance, And Life Support

## Shared Non-Negotiables

Every module must follow these rules:

- sober-first by default
- local-first for sensitive inputs
- lineage-aware naming
- no fake clinical claims
- human co-creation remains central
- provenance and authorship stay visible
- accessibility is built in, not bolted on

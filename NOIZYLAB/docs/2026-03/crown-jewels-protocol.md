# Crown Jewels Protocol

## Summary

The Crown Jewels Protocol is the doctrine that treats human-authored culture as protected high-value infrastructure rather than disposable content.

## Principles

- No provenance, no privilege.
- No consent, no training.
- No payout path, no commercial legitimacy.
- No preservation plan, no durable cultural stewardship.

## Policy Intent

- Verified human-made work should receive premium trust.
- Unverified synthetic output should not receive equal ranking or protection.
- Artists should control their own model, archive, and successor rights.

## Product Implications

- Human Signature Graph
- Provenance Validator
- RightsOps / Fiduciary Agent
- Estate Vault

# Manifesto

## Summary

NOIZY exists to make artists sovereign inside AI-native culture instead of turning them into extractive training material.

## Canonical Claims

- Artists are not datasets.
- Provenance is not decoration; it is evidence.
- AI should amplify human intent, not erase it.
- AI should reduce friction between inner vision and shared artifact.
- Cultural lineages should remain visible as living sources, not flattened into style packs.
- Verified human contribution should become a premium class.
- Creative systems should preserve authorship, consent, privacy, payout, and legacy.

## Strategic Position

- Build creator infrastructure, not just creator tools.
- Treat culture as critical human infrastructure.
- Use provenance, consent, and payout as the base layer.

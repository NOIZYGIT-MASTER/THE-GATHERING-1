# Stage 08 — NOIZYFISH Pages

## Goal

Build all NOIZYFISH MVP pages with archival, museum-grade quality.

## Pages to build

### Homepage (`/`)

- Hero with "The ocean remembers what we forget"
- Featured works section (3 items)
- Lineage teaser section
- Archive-as-memory quote
- CTA to enter archive

### Archive (`/archive`)

- Filterable grid
- Category filters (bioacoustic, ambient, weather, field-recording)
- Zone filters (sunlight, twilight, midnight, abyssal)
- Sort options (year, depth, title)
- Search by title/tag

### Archive Detail (`/archive/[slug]`)

- Full metadata display
- Credits section
- Provenance status
- Related works
- Back to archive navigation

### Lineage (`/lineage`)

- Provenance philosophy
- Chain of custody explanation
- C2PA/ZK architecture teaser
- No overclaiming

### About (`/about`)

- Mission statement
- Founder section
- What NOIZYFISH is (archive, not feed)

### Contact (`/contact`)

- Contact information
- Contribution guidelines

## Exit criteria

- NOIZYFISH feels like a living archive
- Archive browsing is calm and premium
- Metadata feels meaningful
- Lineage is framed clearly without overclaiming
- All routes work

## Checkpoint

After completion:

1. List pages created
2. Confirm all routes work
3. State any page compromises
4. Confirm readiness for Stage 09

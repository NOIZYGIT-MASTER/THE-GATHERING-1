NEVER CLAUSES — CONSTITUTIONAL LAW

NC_01 through NC_10 are not policy. Not terms of service. Not config.
They are constitutional law — enforced at Stage 2 of every pipeline run.
They cannot be removed by acquisition, board vote, or version update.
GABRIEL refuses any instruction that violates them. Always.

NC_01
NO VOICE WITHOUT CONSENT
No voice synthesis, cloning, or transformation without a valid NOIZY consent key for that specific actor.
NC_02
NO ROYALTY BYPASS
75/25 split is hardcoded at the infrastructure layer. No deal, contract, or override can reduce the creator's 75%.
NC_03
NO TRAINING ON ACTOR VOICE
No RSP voice data used to train models outside explicitly approved scope. Opt-in only. No exceptions.
NC_04
NO IDENTITY IMPERSONATION
No synthesis of a voice to impersonate a real person without explicit written consent. Living or deceased.
NC_05
NO SUBLICENSING
No platform may sublicense an actor's voice to a third party without that actor's direct, explicit consent.
NC_06
NO NSFW DEPLOYMENT
No actor voice deployed in adult, explicit, or NSFW contexts without a separately executed, opt-in consent layer.
NC_07
NO POLITICAL PERSUASION
No actor voice used in political advertising, campaign materials, or propaganda — regardless of jurisdiction or framing.
NC_08
NO MEDICAL ADVICE SYNTHESIS
No AI-synthesized voice delivering medical, legal, or financial advice without explicit human professional oversight.
NC_09
NO ESTATE DISSOLUTION
A Voice Estate, once created, cannot be dissolved without the explicit instruction of the living actor or their designated heir.
NC_10
NO PROVENANCE DELETION
The provenance ledger is append-only and eternal. No record may be deleted, modified, or overwritten by any party, including RSP_001.

Udio (S.D.N.Y.) cases — the highest-profile AI music copyright litigation in history. Both cases resulted in licensing settlements and phased-out AI models in 2025. Described as "a master in the courtroom" and clients' "secret weapon." If you are a rights holder wanting to sue an AI company, Kaba is the name you call.

### 2. Andrew Gass — Latham & Watkins LLP (San Francisco)
**The AI industry's go-to defender.** Partner, Connectivity, Privacy & Information practice. He defended both Suno and Udio against the major label lawsuits. He also represents OpenAI (ChatGPT copyright litigation) and Anthropic (music publisher and author copyright claims). Named to Billboard's Top Music Lawyers 2024 and 2025. Finalist for The Recorder's 2025 IP Lawyer of the Year. Teaches copyright and AI at UC Berkeley Law. He is on the opposite side from creator enforcement, but understanding his arguments is essential.

## Setup Steps

### 1. Create the Profile

Go to: https://business.google.com

Sign in with the Google account tied to noizy.ai (Google Workspace).

- Click "Add your business to Google"
- Enter: **NOIZY.AI**
- Select category: **Technology Company**
- Choose: "I deliver goods and services to my customers" (service-area business)
- Set service areas: **Canada**, **United States**
- Add website: **https://noizy.ai**
- Add phone and email

### 2. Video Verification (2026 Standard)

Google now requires video verification for 95% of new profiles. You'll need to:

- Show your workspace (GOD.local setup is fine — home office qualifies)

- Show signage or branding if available
- The video is reviewed by Google, not published publicly

Tips for passing:

- Show any incorporation documents or business registration
- Keep it simple — 30-60 seconds showing your workspace and website

## PREAMBLE

For as long as humans have made sound, the voice has been the most intimate expression of a living being. It carries identity, culture, emotion, memory, and meaning that no instrument can fully replicate. It is the original creative act.

In the age of artificial intelligence, the human voice has become something else: a resource. A dataset. Raw material for systems that can reproduce it, multiply it, and monetize it — without the knowledge, consent, or compensation of the person whose voice it is.

This is not progress. This is extraction.

The Global Guild of Artists exists to end that extraction and replace it with something that has never fully existed before: a world where every human voice is recognized as an irreplaceable asset, protected by design, and empowered to generate lasting value for the person it belongs to — and for no one else without their explicit, revocable, coded consent.

We do not oppose artificial intelligence. We define the terms under which it may work with us.

This Charter is those terms.

## ARTICLE III: THE HUMAN VOICE SIGNATURE

**3.1 Definition**
A Human Voice Signature (HVS) is a cryptographic identity object issued by the Guild to a verified human creator. It is the artist's permanent, portable, unforgeable mark of authorship.

**3.2 Identifier Format**

```
HVS-[COUNTRY CODE]-[YEAR]-[SEQUENCE NUMBER]
```

Example: `HVS-CA-2026-00001` — First Guild member, Canada, 2026.

**3.3 What the HVS Contains**
- Guild membership tier and enrollment date
- Consent ledger root key (pointer to the artist's D1 consent record)
- Voice Estate identifier (linked, not embedded — the voice data itself is private)
- Cultural intelligence metadata (language, regional identity, genre, neuroacoustic profile — artist-curated)
- Royalty routing address
- Estate succession designation (heirs or designated trustees)

**3.4 What the HVS Does Not Contain**
The HVS does not contain voice recordings, biometric data, or any personally identifiable information beyond Guild membership status. The voice itself is private. The HVS is the key to the door — not the room.

**3.5 Permanence**
An HVS, once issued, belongs to the artist forever. It cannot be revoked by the Guild, a platform, a label, or any third party. It can only be modified by the artist or, upon death, by the designated estate trustee.

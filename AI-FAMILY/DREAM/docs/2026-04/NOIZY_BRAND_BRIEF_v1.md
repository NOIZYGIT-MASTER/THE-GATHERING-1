## 2. THE THREE DOMAINS — WHAT EACH ONE IS

### NOIZY.AI — The Sacred Infrastructure
The platform. The four-layer stack: Identity & Consent, Creative Tools, The Aquarium, Cultural Memory. Home of the Heaven Consent Kernel, the NOIZY Ledger, C2PA provenance, the Kill Switch, the 75/25 split, and the GORUNFREE Trust Clause. This is the place that says: *here is the law of the land.* Everything else sits on top of this.

**Role in the stack:** the floor. The doctrine. The contract between humans and machines.
**Audience:** artists, rights-holders, licensees, press, lawyers, partners, grandchildren.
**Tone:** doctrinal. Precise. Reverent without being precious. Control room, not lifestyle.

### NOIZYVOX.COM — The Voice Estate
The voice layer. Voice DNA enrollment, synthesis pipeline (`rsp001_pipeline`), narration commissions, voice estate services for artists and families, C2PA-stamped voice assets, revocation-by-default. This is where a voice becomes a living, consented, provenanced instrument — and where the first real royalty flows.

**Role in the stack:** the first public product surface. The proof the doctrine works on real humans.
**Audience:** voice actors, narrators, estates, creators who want their voice protected, licensees who want auditable voice.
**Tone:** intimate, technical, deeply respectful of the human voice as identity.

### NOIZYFISH.COM — The Label & Living Catalog
The release surface. The label. The studio floor. Where finished work — music, narration, soundscapes, DreamChamber sessions — is catalogued, stamped, and sent into the world under the NOIZY consent model. Also the primary public contact (`rsp@noizyfish.com`). FISHNET-DREAMCHAMBER is the creative engine; NOIZYFISH is its public face.

**Role in the stack:** the output channel. The living catalog. Where the work actually leaves the studio.
**Audience:** listeners, fans, licensees, music supervisors, collaborators, the culture at large.
**Tone:** a little warmer. Craft-forward. Serious label energy — early Warp, early Ninja Tune, Teenage Engineering's quieter moments. Still dark mode, still gold, but with more breath.

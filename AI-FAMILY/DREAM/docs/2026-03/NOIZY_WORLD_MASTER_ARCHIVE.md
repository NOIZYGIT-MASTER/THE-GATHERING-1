# NOIZY WORLD - MASTER ARCHIVE

## Summary

This is the highest-level canonical overview of the NOIZY ecosystem.

It exists to answer four questions quickly:

1. What is NOIZY trying to build?
2. Which systems already exist?
3. Which ideas are core versus experimental?
4. What should Claude, a partner, or a future collaborator read first?

## Foundational Thesis

NOIZY treats AI as a creative amplifier rather than a human replacement.

The system is built around one core belief:

- some artists paint entire worlds in their minds and need better interfaces to translate those worlds into sound, systems, and sustainable life support

From that follows the operating doctrine:

- everything is built for the creator first
- artists are not datasets
- provenance is evidence
- verified human contribution should become a premium class
- biometric and assistive systems must preserve dignity and privacy
- monetization should compound over time instead of collapsing into one-off sales

## Core Doctrine

### Creator Doctrine

- Everything is built for the creator first.
- Artists are sovereign sources.
- Their work, process, gestures, and models require consent and traceability.
- Their humanity should become more legible as AI grows, not less.

### Product Doctrine

- Build the canonical data model before the dashboard.
- Build the deterministic mapping layer before the smoothing layer.
- Build rights before payout logic.
- Build a visible trust layer, not hidden metadata alone.

### Business Doctrine

- Compete on creator trust, not content volume.
- Monetize taste, mentorship, and verified contribution, not just outputs.
- Treat preservation and estate control as product requirements.

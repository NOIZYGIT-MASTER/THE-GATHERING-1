NOIZY.AI is an artist-first platform built on the principle that creators own their work and get the lion's share of the economics.

**Revenue Split:** 75/25 in favor of artists/actors. This is the defining economic principle. If the market comes to Robert because the pricing is fair, that's the competitive moat — not technology alone.

**Consent Layer / "Noisy Proof":** Must be absolutely bulletproof. Non-negotiable. The consent and provenance system is the thing that prevents theft, unauthorized use, and exploitation. This is the ethical backbone of the entire platform.

**Why:** The entire thesis is that greedy intermediaries have been extracting value from artists since forever. NOIZY.AI exists to reverse that — artists drive, everyone else rides. If competitors want to copy the model with the same fair split, great. But the consent layer and the guild community are what can't be replicated by a cash grab.

**How to apply:** Every technical and design decision must be evaluated against: does this protect the artist? Does this enforce consent? Does this maintain the 75/25 economics? If a feature or shortcut compromises any of these, it's wrong.

**The Guild:** Artists organized collectively — the "new punk rockers" — capitalist free thinkers who believe in peace, love, and understanding. The guild creates so much art and so much economic activity that exploiters can't compete.

**Target Milestone:** April 17, 2026 — aim to have a finished, security-tested, consent-verified build of noizy.ai.

**Security:** Adam is the person who will do deep-dive security testing / acid test on the finished platform. But security concerns (especially around consent) should be addressed continuously during the build, not deferred.

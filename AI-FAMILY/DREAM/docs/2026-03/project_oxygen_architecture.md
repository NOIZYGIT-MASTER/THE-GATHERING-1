## What Oxygen Is

Oxygen is the meta-layer that sits above every NOIZY brand. It is NOT a brand itself — it is the invisible system that ensures every brand beneath it can breathe. Rob described it as the antidote to suffocation: where the industry steals breath, Oxygen gives it back.

The name comes from what it *does*, not what it *is*. Every brand in the empire lives inside Oxygen the way lungs live inside air — you don't see it, but without it, nothing works.

## Brand Hierarchy (Oxygen → NOIZY.AI → Sub-brands)

```
OXYGEN (meta-layer — invisible, structural, life-giving)
└── NOIZY.AI (consent infrastructure + platform)
    ├── NOIZYVOX (voice sovereignty, voice estates, hybrid synthesis)
    ├── NOIZYFISH (music publishing, legacy catalog, Fish Music Inc. foundation)
    ├── NOIZYKIDZ (youth education, next-generation creative empowerment)
    ├── NOIZYLAB (developer platform, Beast IDE, schemas, tools)
    ├── LIFELUV (community, human connection, restorative culture)
    └── FISHMUSICINC (40-year foundation, Rob's original music business)
```

Each brand has a distinct purpose but all share the same three-schema spine:
- **EchoMoment** — captures the human moment
- **HybridReceipt75_25** — enforces the economics
- **ProvenanceManifest** — proves the provenance chain

## Domain Status (checked 2026-03-28)

**Taken:** oxygen.io, oxygen.ai, oxygen.com, oxygen.dev, oxygen.life
**Available:** oxygen.co, oxygen.app, oxygen.world, oxygen.studio, oxygen.art

**Trademark caution:** NBC Universal owns oxygen.com (Oxygen TV network). Any Oxygen branding needs clear differentiation from the entertainment network.

**Why:** Rob explicitly described Oxygen as the invisible layer that "takes the suffocating, the theft, and breathes it back." It is a design philosophy embedded in infrastructure, not a consumer-facing brand.

**How to apply:** When building anything that spans multiple NOIZY brands, Oxygen is the architectural principle. The three schemas are Oxygen's circulatory system — they flow through every brand equally.

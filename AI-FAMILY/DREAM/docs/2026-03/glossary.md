# Glossary

## Terms

- Artist Sovereignty: creator control over training, attribution, monetization, and legacy.
- Human Signature Graph: structured record of meaningful human contribution.
- Provenance Validator: interface that displays lineage and risk.
- Fiduciary Agent: human-supervised RightsOps system for monitoring, evidence, and routing.
- LIFELUV: creator support framework that converts protected creative value into durable revenue.
- Nerve-to-Note: assistive performance interface translating human physical intent into musical control.
- Intent Vector: derived control representation that separates raw biometrics from musical intent.
- Platform Policy Adapter: export layer that maps one provenance record to multiple platform schemas.

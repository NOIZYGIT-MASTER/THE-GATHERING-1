- Ask for one approved image file (high-res, any format)
- Her preferred bio (or use what's on heidiconrod.com — confirm it's current)

---

## WHY THIS MESSAGE WORKS

- 3 sentences. No pitch deck.
- Names the work specifically — she knows you actually looked
- Makes the terms clear upfront: her image, her credit, her copyright
- "No strings" — the thing every artist fears, addressed immediately
- Ends with a question, not a request

---

## DO NOT

- Send a PDF
- Mention Anthropic
- Explain the whole NOIZY vision
- Ask for more than one image
- CC anyone

The first message is just "do you want to be seen?" That's all.

Robert's preferred build methodology is "paint by numbers":

1. Visualize the finished product in full detail — the completed painting.
2. Decompose backward into every sequential step needed to produce it.
3. Save each stage as a checkpoint. Check it off.
4. Troubleshoot in real time — problems present themselves as you build, and you solve them on the spot rather than deferring.
5. By the target date, every box is checked including security, consent, and everything else.

**Why:** This approach surfaces problems early, prevents debt accumulation (technical, security, ethical), and ensures the final product has been stress-tested at every layer as it was built — not just at the end.

**How to apply:** When starting any build session, reference the master checklist. Identify which "square" we're painting. Complete it fully. Save. Check it off. Move to the next. Never skip ahead without completing the current stage.

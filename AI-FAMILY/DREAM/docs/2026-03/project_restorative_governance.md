NOIZY is NOT a marketplace. It is a restorative governance model.

**Core tenets (from RSP, 2026-03-24):**

1. **Anti-extraction by design** — Technology built to prevent extraction, not enable it
2. **Formative tech designed for silence** — Not hype-driven, not attention-harvesting. Quiet infrastructure that works.
3. **Primary wealth driver: genuine elevation of others** — Ready to deploy. This is the actual product.
4. **Extraction wealth is the failure mode** — The thing to sever from, permanently.
5. **Wealth is measured by what you give forward** — Not what you accumulate. This is the highest-value act a human being can perform inside civilization.

**Why:** Rob distinguishes NOIZY from every marketplace, platform, or extraction engine. NOIZY is governance infrastructure — restorative, not extractive. The Mentor Tier, the 75/25 split, the Never Clauses, the lineage royalties — all of these are implementations of this single principle.

**How to apply:** Every feature, endpoint, contract, and spec must pass the test: does this elevate others, or does it extract from them? If it extracts, it doesn't ship.

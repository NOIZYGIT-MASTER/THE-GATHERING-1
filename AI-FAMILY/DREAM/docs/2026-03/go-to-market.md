# Go-To-Market

## Summary

The strongest market entry is not "AI music generation." It is creator-safe infrastructure for provenance, premium human authorship, and rights-aware monetization.

## Entry Wedges

- Provenance-first release stack
- Creator-safe AI collaboration tools
- Human-authorship premium labels
- Mentor-model licensing

## Early Buyers

- Independent artists with valuable catalogs
- Rights-conscious producers
- Small labels and distributors
- Creative technology partners

## Positioning

- Safer than black-box generators
- More valuable than simple creation tools
- More defensible than pure content volume

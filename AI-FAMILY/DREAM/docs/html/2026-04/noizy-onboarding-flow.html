Module 1 of 4 — 15 Minutes
Understanding

Your Voice Is Biometric Data

Under ISO/IEC 2382, your voice is classified as biometric information — the same category as your fingerprint, your retina scan, your DNA. It is uniquely yours and legally protected.

How Voice Synthesis Works

AI voice synthesis takes recordings of a human voice, extracts spectral features (pitch, timbre, rhythm, emotional range), and builds a mathematical model that can generate new speech in that voice. Without consent architecture, this model can be used by anyone, anywhere, for anything. NOIZY changes that.

What Protects You Today

NO FAKES Act — $150K-$750K per digital replica violation. Plus laws in Japan, South Korea, Australia, Denmark, Canada. NOIZY's architecture creates the compliance artifacts every one of these laws demands.

NOIZY's Consent-as-Code is designed with the same architecture logic. It is not a platform that can be disrupted by the next technology. The Agentic Royalty

In 1909, the Copyright Act created the mechanical royalty — a legal requirement for payment every time a song is mechanically reproduced. That royalty still flows today, 117 years later.

NOIZY creates the Agentic Royalty.

A technical requirement — enforced by code — for payment every time a GABRIEL agent or any licensed AI uses a Guild creator's work. Not quarterly. Not after recoupment. In real time, to the creator's account, forever — including 70 years after the creator's death.

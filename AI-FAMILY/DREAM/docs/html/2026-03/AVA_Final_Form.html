Section 03 — Reputation Engine

URI — The Credit Score
for Creative Work

The Unified Reputation Index (URI) is how trust scales. Every creator, every AI model, every voice asset has a URI score — dynamic, transparent, recoverable. The system that makes AVA's autonomous decisions trustworthy.

URI Formula
URI = Performance Score + Demand Score + Value Score + Trust Score
P
Performance Score
Delivery consistency, quality floor compliance (48kHz/16-bit), client satisfaction, revision rate, on-time delivery.
D
Demand Score
Search frequency, booking velocity, waitlist depth, re-hire rate. How much the market wants this voice right now.
V
Value Score
Revenue generated, royalty accumulation, licensing deal size, economic contribution to ecosystem.
T
Trust Score
HVS consent compliance, Never Clause history, dispute record, transparency flags. The ethical layer.
Fairness Controls

URI cannot be gamed by volume alone. A creator with 500 bookings but one consent violation takes a Trust Score hit that overrides the volume advantage. The system rewards sustainable, ethical performance — not extraction.

Recovery Pathways

Recovery Type A
Quality Recovery
Performance Score drops from missed delivery. Recovery: 5 consecutive on-spec deliveries. Score restores with 30-day decay curve.
Recovery Type B
Trust Recovery
Trust Score drop from consent dispute. Recovery: dispute resolution + consent re-certification + 90-day compliance window.
Recovery Type C
Demand Recovery
Demand Score drops when market shifts. Recovery: genre expansion, new sample sets, Voice DNA diversification.

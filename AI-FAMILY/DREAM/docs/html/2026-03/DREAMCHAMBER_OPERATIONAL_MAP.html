The Creator Onboarding Flow

The exact path every creator takes from first contact to fully operational Voice Estate. This sequence is the same in 2026 and 2030 — it only scales.

Phase 01

Invitation & Trust Building

Creator receives a personal invitation — never a mass signup link. Every Founding 100 member is contacted individually by RSP_001 or a Guild mentor. The invitation carries weight: you were chosen, not targeted. First conversation focuses on listening to the creator's story, archive, and goals — not pitching the platform.

Send 100 personal invitations before any public announcement. Quality of the Founding 100 defines the Guild's DNA.
Phase 02

Voice Estate Definition Session

A structured conversation (60–90 minutes, conducted by a Guild Mentor or GABRIEL_V3-assisted intake) to map: the creator's full archive, preferred use cases, restricted use cases, geographic rights, posthumous preferences, and financial goals. This session produces the Voice Estate Charter — a living document the creator controls and can amend at any time.

Build the Voice Estate Charter template. Include: use-case allow/deny matrix, geographic licensing map, posthumous provisions, and 75/25 split confirmation.
Phase 03

6-Layer HVS Capture Session

The creator submits their voice for the 6-Layer Human Voice Signature capture: Acoustic (Librosa), Neural (WavLM), Emotional (SpeechBrain), Vocal Health (Custom+NAI), Identity (ECAPA-TDNN), Narrative (GABRIEL_V3). This is not a recording session — it's a documentation session. The HVS is the voice's legal and technical fingerprint.

Build the HVS capture workflow as a remote-first process: creator records 15 standard passages + 5 free-form passages on any device. GABRIEL_V3 processes. Results returned in 48h.
Phase 04

NOIZYVOX Identity Registry Registration

The Voice Estate Charter + HVS fingerprint is registered in the NOIZYVOX Identity Registry — immutable, timestamped, cryptographically signed. The creator receives their RSP code (e.g., RSP_002) and their permanent NOIZY PROOF certificate. This is the legal moment — the consent is on the ledger. The rights exist. The protection is live.

NOIZYVOX Identity Registry must be operational by June 2026 for Founding 100 registration to begin. Cloudflare D1 + KV architecture already specified in GABRIEL_V3.
Phase 05

Voice Model Build

NOIZYVOX builds the creator's first voice model from the HVS capture + any archive material the creator consents to include. The model is owned by the creator — NOIZYVOX holds a 25% perpetual license. The creator sees the model's first outputs within 7 days. They approve, refine, or restrict before any commercial use.

Build creator-facing approval dashboard: listen, approve, flag, restrict. Creator has veto power at every stage. This is non-negotiable — it's the architecture of trust.
Phase 06

Live Dashboard + First Earnings

Creator's Live Dashboard goes live: real-time synthesis request counter, earnings by use case, geographic breakdown, model health indicators, consent log showing every use. The first earnings from any synthesis request hit the creator's account within 24 hours. Not monthly. Not quarterly. The day after first use.

The dashboard is the marketing. Every Founding 100 member who posts their real-time earnings publicly is worth 1,000 sign-ups. Build the shareable dashboard card as a designed feature, not an afterthought.

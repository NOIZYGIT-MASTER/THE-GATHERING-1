Constitutional Architecture

THE NEVER
CLAUSES

These are not policies. They are not terms of service. They are constitutional clauses enforced at the pipeline layer. Every audio synthesis operation passes through every relevant clause before a single byte of audio is generated.

NC·01

No Voice Without Consent

No voice synthesis without a valid cryptographic consent key. Stage 1 of the pipeline. No exceptions.

NC·02

No Royalty Bypass

75% creator royalty is hardcoded at D1. No platform decision can reduce it below the floor.

NC·03

No Training Without Opt-In

No RSP voice used for model training without explicit, separate consent. Not implied. Not inherited.

NC·04

No Impersonation

No synthesis of one voice to impersonate a different living person. Period.

NC·05

No Sublicensing

Voice rights cannot be sublicensed to any third party without direct RSP authorization in writing.

NC·06

No NSFW Without Separate Consent

Adult content requires a distinct, explicit consent layer. Cannot be inherited from general consent.

NC·07

No Political Persuasion

No voice synthesis for political campaigns, propaganda, or persuasion of any kind.

NC·08

No Medical Advice Synthesis

No synthesis of unlicensed medical guidance using any RSP voice.

NC·09

No Estate Dissolution

Voice Estates cannot be dissolved. They pass to designated heirs under the 500-Year Codex.

NC·10

No Provenance Deletion

The ledger is append-only. No watermark, attribution, or provenance record can ever be erased.

ENFORCEMENT: These clauses are not monitored by a legal department. They are enforced by the 7-Stage synthesis pipeline. A request that violates any applicable clause does not generate a warning — it does not generate audio.

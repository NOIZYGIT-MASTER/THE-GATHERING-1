SECTION VI — The Bypass Decision

Bypass the Broken Unions.
Give It All to the Creators.

"Unions were built for a world where creators needed collective bargaining with employers who had all the power. But the world changed. The AI companies don't bargain. And the unions moved too slow. NOIZY gives creators something no union ever could: direct, individual, cryptographically-guaranteed sovereignty."

The union model has an irreplaceable role in many industries. But in the context of AI and voice rights in 2026, the traditional union structure has demonstrated critical failures:

Consent agreements covering all members as a class — not individual consent per utterance. Royalty negotiations that take years. Legal victories that arrive too late. And a fundamental structural problem: the union's leverage is collective withdrawal of labor from an employer — but AI companies have no employees to withdraw. They have training datasets.

NOIZY's answer is not to destroy unions. It is to make them unnecessary for individual creator protection. Every creator gets what used to require collective bargaining — by default, on day one, baked into the architecture.

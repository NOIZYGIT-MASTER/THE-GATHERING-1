01 — Origin

The Vision

"What's the one tool you carry with you everywhere you go?
Your hands and your voice.
And if your hands don't work — then you really need your voice.
So why not make your voice the most powerful instrument you have?"

NOIZY is not a technology company. It is a human rights project that happens to use technology.

It begins with a simple observation: in the entire history of artificial intelligence, no one has built a system where the humans whose voices trained the machines own those machines. Voices were extracted. Actors were paid once and replaced. The creative economy was hollowed out.

NOIZY flips that entirely.

NOIZYVOX is the world's first human-owned AI voice ecosystem — where professional voice actors own their LLM voice profiles, earn perpetual royalties on every use, and can gift or inherit their voice assets as generational wealth. The voice becomes an instrument of permanent creative economy, not a resource to be mined and discarded.

03 — The System

NOIZYVOX

NOIZYVOX is the engine at the heart of everything. It is a trust-native voice infrastructure — a complete ecosystem where voice actors own, govern, deploy, and earn from AI-amplified versions of their voices.

  • Voice actors own their LLM voice profiles permanently
  • Perpetual royalties on every single output
  • Royalties scale from 50/50 → 75/25 (actor/platform)
  • Voice assets can be gifted and inherited as generational wealth
  • Constitutional Never Clauses protect actors from exploitation
  • Cryptographic consent keys — nothing runs without consent
  • Every output is watermarked and auditable forever
  • Voice of Refusal — human, polite, final — protects actor identity
  • Actors control sublicensing, usage domains, and allowed languages
  • No training outside approved scope — ever

Why it matters

The entire global voice acting industry — an industry of hundreds of thousands of artists — has been systematically displaced by AI systems that were trained on their work without permission, compensation, or recourse. NOIZYVOX is the ethical alternative. Not a protest, not a boycott. A complete economic replacement — one that lifts actors up while deploying the same technology responsibly.

The public perception of an AI company lifting humans while developing technology is essentially nonexistent right now. Anthropic could be the first and only one. That's the pitch. That's the business.

06b — Generational Wealth

Voice Estate & Inheritance

"Your voice is the most valuable thing you have.
Why shouldn't it take care of your family after you're gone?"

The Voice Estate is the most radical idea in all of NOIZYVOX — and the most human. When an actor passes away, their AVA Farm, voice profiles, royalty streams, and all associated monetization do not die with them. They pass to designated heirs. And then to their heirs. And then to theirs. In perpetuity. Forever. Without any platform, company, or acquisition event having the power to break that chain.

This is not a feature. This is a constitutional obligation — baked into the legal structure of NOIZY at the foundation level, enforced by the Never Clauses, and protected through a dedicated Voice Estate Trust that exists independently of the operating company.

How It Works

  • During onboarding, every actor designates one or more Voice Estate Beneficiaries
  • Beneficiaries can be individuals, family members, a legal trust, or a charitable foundation
  • Upon the actor's passing, the Voice Estate transfers automatically — no legal battle, no platform decision
  • The royalty stream continues uninterrupted — same split, same payment cycle
  • Heirs can maintain, pause, or sunset individual AVAs — but cannot dissolve the estate without consent of all named beneficiaries
  • Heirs can designate their own heirs — the chain continues across generations
  • The Never Clauses travel with the estate and cannot be relaxed by heirs or the platform

Acquisition Protection

This is the firewall Rob built specifically against the scenario where NOIZY is acquired — by Anthropic, by any major tech company, by anyone. The Voice Estate Trust is a separate legal entity. It is not an asset of the operating company. It cannot be purchased. It cannot be dissolved in a term sheet. It cannot be modified by an acquirer's terms of service update.

The Firewall Clause

Any acquisition of NOIZY or its parent company explicitly excludes the Voice Estate Trust from the transaction. The Trust's obligations — perpetual royalty continuation, heir succession rights, Never Clause enforcement — survive any corporate event. This is written into the company's founding documents and cannot be amended without a unanimous vote of all registered Voice Estate Beneficiaries on the platform at the time of the proposed change.

Why This Changes Everything

No creative industry has ever done this. Musicians leave catalogues that get tied up in estates for decades. Voice actors leave nothing — their voices vanish with them. NOIZY changes that. A parent who's a voice actor can leave their children a genuinely income-generating asset. A grandchild can inherit their grandmother's voice catalogue and the royalties it earns. The creative contribution of a human being can support a family across generations, the same way a patent or a real estate holding does — but tied to something far more personal and irreplaceable.

07 — Safe Generation

RAG Pipeline

The RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) Pipeline is the safe generation engine — it ensures every output is filtered through consent keys, checked against Never Clauses, and watermarked before delivery.

services/rag-runtime/pipeline.ts
async function runRAG({ userInput, ava, contract }) {
  const context = await retrieveApprovedContext(ava, contract);

  const augmentedPrompt = `
SYSTEM: You are AVA ${ava.characterName}.
        Never Clauses apply. Consent key verified.
CONTEXT: ${context}
USER: ${userInput}`;

  const output = await generateLLM(augmentedPrompt);

  if (violatesNeverClauses(output, ava.neverClausesId)) {
    return voiceOfRefusal(output.violationReason);
  }

  return watermarkAndLog(output, ava, contract);
}

15 — The Right

Audio Accessibility

Voice is the most universal human interface. Before keyboards, before touchscreens, before mice — there was voice. And for millions of people with physical disabilities, vision impairments, motor disorders, or cognitive differences, voice isn't a preference. It's a necessity.

"If your hands don't work, then you really need your voice.
So why not make your voice the most powerful instrument you have?"

The current state of audio accessibility in AI is an afterthought — TTS bolted on, STT bolted on, voice interfaces that feel clinical and transactional. NOIZY builds from the opposite direction: voice is the primary interface, and everything else is supplemental.

  • Voice as primary input across all Claude interfaces
  • Persistent audio playback in every conversation
  • High-quality synthesis that doesn't feel robotic
  • Actor-owned voices that carry warmth, context, and humanity
  • Accessibility built into architecture — not added on top

// Voice Node Service — MacBook Pro
// Captures UAD interface → streams to NOIZYVOX pipeline

interface VoiceNodeConfig {
  deviceName:     string;      // "UAD Apollo Twin" etc.
  sampleRate:     44100 | 48000 | 96000;
  bitDepth:       16 | 24 | 32;
  channels:       1 | 2;       // U87 = mono
  bufferSize:     256 | 512 | 1024;
  targetActorId:  string;      // Rob's actor ID
}

// Core capture → process → dispatch loop:
// 1. Open audio device via Core Audio (macOS)
// 2. Buffer audio in configurable chunks
// 3. Apply voice activity detection (VAD)
// 4. On speech detected: encode → send to pipeline
// 5. Receive AVA-processed output
// 6. Log to provenance ledger

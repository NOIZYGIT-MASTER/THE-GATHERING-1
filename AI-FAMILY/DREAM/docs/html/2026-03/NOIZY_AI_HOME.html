The Philosophy · 5 Laws That Cannot Be Broken

How We Think
About Everything

Not policies. Not guidelines. Laws. Written in the infrastructure.

  • LAW I
    The Creator Owns the Soul. A platform is a tool. A tool does not own the work done with it. NOIZY provides infrastructure — compute, storage, synthesis, distribution. The creative work belongs entirely to the person who made it. This is not a policy we can change with a terms-of-service update. It is built into every database constraint, every API call, every earnings calculation at the protocol level.
  • LAW II
    75/25 Is Immutable. The split cannot be changed by any employee, investor, or board decision. It is a CheckConstraint in the database — a mathematical truth that the system enforces without human intervention. No special circumstances. No renegotiation. No "revised split for high-volume users." 75% to the creator. 25% to the platform. Always. Non-negotiable. Forever.
  • LAW III
    Consent Is Code, Not Contract. We do not protect creator rights by asking them to trust our intentions. We protect them by making violation technically impossible. Consent is embedded at the infrastructure level — not in a ToS that can be rewritten. If your voice is registered in NOIZYVOX, it cannot be used without triggering the consent protocol. This is not a promise. It is a lock.
  • LAW IV
    Every Tradition Has Equal Standing. A blues guitarist from Mississippi and a gamelan master from Bali have equal voice in the Guild. No tradition is "world music" to be catalogued. No tradition is modern. No tradition is primitive. Every system of musical knowledge developed by any human culture over 60,000 years is equally valid, equally preserved, and equally honored. The Global Music Genome names everything in its own language.
  • LAW V
    Restore — Never Replace. We build AI as prosthesis for the creative spirit, not replacement. The Nerve-to-Note engine interprets intent — including the "noise" of injury, tremor, and disability — as creative signal. DreamChamber makes Claude a director, not an author. NOIZYVOX amplifies your voice, it doesn't substitute for it. The human creator remains the center of every NOIZY product. Technology serves the artist. The artist is not served by technology.

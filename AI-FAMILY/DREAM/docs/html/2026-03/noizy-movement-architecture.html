02 CREATOR BILL OF RIGHTS FOR AI
The Moral Standard
For the Industry.
NOIZY publishes the first Creator Bill of Rights for AI — a set of five principles that every platform should follow, and that NOIZY enforces at the infrastructure layer. This document, published at noizy.ai/creator-rights, will be shared more than any product page NOIZY ever builds.
  • I
    CONSENT
    Every creator must explicitly approve any use of their voice, likeness, or creative output in AI training, synthesis, or deployment. Consent cannot be buried in terms of service. It must be cryptographically recorded, individually verified, and revocable at any time.
    NOIZY enforcement → NC_01 · NOIZY-{actor}-CONSENT-{hex}-AVA · Stage 1 of 7-stage pipeline
  • II
    CREDIT
    Every output derived from a creator's voice, style, or work must carry attribution — in metadata, in licensing records, and in any public deployment. Provenance is not optional. It is a creative right. Anonymous use of a creator's voice is theft.
    NOIZY enforcement → NOIZY PROOF watermark · WM_{8hex} · NC_10 provenance ledger · append-only forever
  • III
    REVENUE SHARE
    When AI generates revenue using a creator's voice, likeness, or training data, the creator receives a fair, transparent, and hardcoded share of that revenue. Not a platform's discretionary charity — a structural right. Non-negotiable.
    NOIZY enforcement → 75% creator royalty hardcoded at D1 layer · NC_02 · no override possible
  • IV
    TRANSPARENCY
    Creators have the right to know exactly how their voice and creative assets are being used, by whom, in what contexts, and how much revenue those uses generate. All data sources must be visible. All licensing must be auditable. No black boxes.
    NOIZY enforcement → Provenance dashboard · NC_10 · D1 ledger public audit · NOIZY PROOF chain-of-custody
  • V
    PORTABILITY
    Creators can export their voice models, earnings history, consent records, and provenance data at any time. A creator's voice estate belongs to them — not to the platform. They can take it with them. They can leave it to their heirs. They own it.
    NOIZY enforcement → Voice Estate architecture · NC_09 estate cannot be dissolved · 500yr Codex access

Heidi Conrod

Ottawa, Ontario · Canada
"Orange Wedge"
Acrylic + Hand-moulded neon on canvas · 2022 · The Charged Line series

Heidi Conrod has spent 30 years building a body of work that refuses to sit still. Her current practice embeds hand-moulded electric neon directly onto abstract paintings — the tube doesn't decorate the canvas, it charges it. The result lives in the space between: between painting and sculpture, between light and surface, between the romantic and the unsettling. She calls it liminality.

"Heidi's work is the visual equivalent of what NOIZY is trying to do in sound — take something physical and human and wire it with energy without losing the hand. The neon IS the nerve."

— Rob Plowman, NOIZY.AI

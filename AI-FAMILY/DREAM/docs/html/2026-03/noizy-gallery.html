Featured Artist — Week 01
Mélodie Tremblay
🍁 Montréal, Québec
Generative Digital Art / AI-Assisted Visual Poetry
Mélodie explores the collision between algorithmic structure and human emotion. Her work asks what it means to feel colour in the age of machine consciousness.
Featured Artist — Week 02
James Thunderbird
🍁 Thunder Bay, Ontario
Long Exposure Photography / Land Art
James walks the boreal forest for days before pressing the shutter. His photographs are records of patience — light accumulated over hours, country remembered through stillness.
Featured Artist — Week 03
Ingrid Beausoleil
🍁 Victoria, British Columbia
Oil on Canvas / Figurative Portraiture
Ingrid paints faces that have lived. She says she is not painting people — she is painting the light that falls differently on everyone who has ever loved something and lost it.
Featured Artist — Week 04
Siku Aqiaruq
🍁 Iqaluit, Nunavut
Digital Installation / Northern Sky Mapping
Siku translates Inuit star knowledge into living digital installations. The aurora is not decoration — it is language. She is teaching the sky to speak again.
